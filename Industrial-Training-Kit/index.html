About Saffron Table

Built around intimate hospitality, vibrant plating, and ingredient-first cooking.

Every detail of our restaurant is crafted to feel elevated yet inviting. From bright small plates and charcoal-grilled mains to polished service and warm lighting, Saffron Table is designed to slow time down.

Seasonal sourcing

FAQ

Helpful details before your visit.

Do you offer vegetarian and vegan options?

Yes. We offer multiple vegetarian dishes and can guide you through vegan-friendly options on request.

Is there a dress code?

Smart casual is recommended. We want guests to feel comfortable while matching the evening atmosphere.

Can I book the restaurant for private events?

Yes. Our private dining area is available for birthdays, corporate dinners, and intimate celebrations.

Do you accommodate allergies?

Absolutely. Please note allergies in your reservation or speak with our team on arrival so we can help safely.
